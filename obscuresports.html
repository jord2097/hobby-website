Sheffield's More Obscure Sports

Sheffield are represented in the Elite Ice Hockey League by the Sheffield Steelers hockey team. They were formed in 1991 and play at the Sheffield Arena, winning numerous league titles and challenge cups.

Sheffield Tigers are a motorcycle speedway team competing in the British SGB Premiership. Their home races take place at the Owlerton Stadium, which is a purpose built speedway track that was built in 1929.

Snowsports has seen popularity in Sheffield mostly recreationally. In 1988 an artificial Ski Village was opened in the Parkwood Springs Area - which unfortunately burned down in 2012. It included a half-pipe, toboggan run and even a downhill biking track.
