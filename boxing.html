The History of Boxing in Sheffield

Sheffield has enjoyed great success in the boxing world especially in the late 20th century. This has been credited to the work of trainer Brendan Ingle and his gym in the Wincobank area. He trained 4 world champions: Johnny Nelson, "Prince" Naseem Hamed, Junior Witter and Kell Brook.
