A Guide to the Diverse Range of Sheffield Sports Teams and their History

Sheffield, throughout history, has been one of the most passionate and diverse cities for sport. There are records of sporting events occuring in Sheffield dating back to the late 1700s. Today, without question it has one of the strongest sporting presences of any city, it has a team for every major competitive sport and even some lesser known ones as well. There are two Sheffield professional League Football clubs in Sheffield United and Sheffield Wednesday. There are top tier ice hockey, basketball and motorcycle speedway teams, as well as a rugby league club which is in the 2nd tier. Multiple boxing world champions such as "Prince" Naseem Hamed call Sheffield a home, as well as cricketer Michael Vaughan and Olympic Champion in heptathlon Jessica Ennis-Hill.
